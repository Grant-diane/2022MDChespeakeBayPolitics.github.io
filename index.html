ABOUT

Computer, Political, and Environmental Science Student

Hailing from Glen Burnie, Maryland, Diane Grant is a Student at Northeastern University pursuing a Political Science and Computer Science Degree with a minor in Environmental Science. In her free time Diane plays ultimate Frisbee, explores the outdoors, enjoys comedy, and finds ways to be civically engaged. While this page is a work in progress, under the blog section current writings can be found on one of Diane's current research areas of interest: the intersection of Chesapeake Bay centric environmentalism and the upcoming 2022 Maryland Gubernatorial. This blog was produced as part of an individual project completed to practice writing skills for an advanced writing class in the summer of 2021.
